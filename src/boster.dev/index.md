## Hello

I am Dave Boster, a software professional in Omaha, Nebraska working across
software architecture, delivery systems, technical collaboration, and the
practice of building useful systems with people.

I care about software delivery experiences that are more human, more effective,
and more achievable. That shows up in clean code, test-driven development,
refactoring, pairing, simple design, release safety, platform modernization, and
the steady work of helping teams make practical progress.
