## Hello

I am a software professional and entrepreneur based in Omaha, Nebraska, working
across software architecture, delivery systems, technical collaboration, and the
practice of building useful systems with people.

I care about shaping software delivery experiences that are more human, more
effective, and more achievable. That shows up when teams work together closely
and stay connected to the people using what they build. It involves simple
design, clean code, test-driven development, and continuous delivery & feedback
cycles.
